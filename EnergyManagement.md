# Energy Management

## What are the activities you do that make you relax - Calm quadrant?
Activities that make me relax in the Calm quadrant include:-
1. Reading a Book
2. Meditating
3. Walking in nature
4. Deep breathing.

These activities help me unwind and rejuvenate my mind and body.


## When do you find getting into the Stress quadrant?
I often find myself entering the Stress quadrant when facing tight work deadlines, learning new and challenging skills, or dealing with unexpected changes in plans. These situations push me to my limits and require extra focus and energy.


## How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?
I understand I'm in the Excitement quadrant when I am enthusiastic and eager to engage in an activity. My heart races slightly, I feel full of energy and feel motivated & inspired.


## Paraphrase the Sleep is your Superpower video in your own words in brief. Only the points, no explanation.
* Sleep improves learning, memory, and decision-making.
* It enhances creativity and problem-solving skills.
* Lack of sleep negatively impacts the immune system.
* Good sleep reduces the risk of various health issues.


## What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?
Some ideas to sleep better include maintaining a consistent sleep schedule, creating a relaxing bedtime routine, reducing screen time before bed, keeping the bedroom dark and cool, and avoiding caffeine or heavy meals close to bedtime.
